# Graffiti Guard Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

Graffiti Guard does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Photos, camera captures, image preprocessing, and graffiti detection remain on the user's device. The app has no analytics, advertising, tracking, account system, or inference server.

Camera and photo-library access are used only when the user chooses an image for detection. Images and detection results are not uploaded by Graffiti Guard.

For privacy questions, contact Pierre-Henry Soria through the project [support page](SUPPORT.md).

This policy will be updated before any future release adds data collection or network-based processing.
