Detect likely graffiti in photos and camera captures without uploading images.

Graffiti Guard runs a bundled Core ML detector entirely on your iPhone or iPad. Choose a photo, take a picture, or try the built-in sample scene. The app highlights likely graffiti and reports confidence for human review.

Key features:
- Private, on-device image processing
- No inference server, account, or internet connection required
- Camera and photo-library support
- Adjustable confidence threshold
- Universal iPhone and iPad interface

Graffiti Guard supports maintenance decisions and should not replace human review.
