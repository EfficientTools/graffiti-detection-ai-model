No sign-in or demo account is required.

To test without granting Photos or Camera access:
1. Tap "Try sample scene."
2. Tap "Detect graffiti."
3. Use "Share report" to inspect the generated text summary.

On iPad, reviewers can also drag an image into the scanner panel. Command-Return runs detection, Command-N starts a new inspection, and Command-Comma opens settings.

The app runs its bundled Core ML model entirely on-device. It does not transmit images, collect user data, use analytics, or contact an inference server. Camera and photo-library access are only used when the reviewer selects those controls.

Results identify likely graffiti in the selected image and require human review. The app does not predict future vandalism.
